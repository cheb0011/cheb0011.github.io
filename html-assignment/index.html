About Me

My name is Soufiane, and I'm from Algeria. I came to Canada in August 1st, 2024. At first, I settled in the city of Montréal for a year and half with the goal of studying at the Université de Montréal UdeM. however, I didn't like the academic system there, so I decided to look for another program. Eventually, I chose to enroll at Algonquin college, after a friend suggested it to me.

I'm currently studying the Interactive Media Design program at AC. This program caught my attention because it encouurages creativity and personal development . It also allows me to interact with innovate and creative people, from whom I can learn and grow. during my studies, I aspire to acquire many skills so that I can become capable of innovation and creative expression.
